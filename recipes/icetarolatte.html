Ice Taro Latte

Recipe and instructions

Here's a recipe for an Iced Taro Latte, a delicious and refreshing beverage you can serve:

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of taro powder
  • 2 shots of espresso (or 1/2 cup of strong brewed coffee)
  • 1 cup of milk (whole milk, almond milk, or any milk of your choice)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup of ice cubes

Instructions

    1. Brew Espresso or Coffe:
    • If using espresso, pull 2 shots and let them cool.
    • If using brewed coffee, make it strong and allow it to cool to room temperature.
    2. Mix Taro Powder:
    • In a small bowl, mix the taro powder with a small amount of hot water to create a smooth paste. This will help the taro powder dissolve better in the drink.
    3. Combine Taro and Espresso (or Coffe):
    • In a separate container, combine the taro paste and the espresso (or coffee). Stir well until the taro paste is fully dissolved.
    4. Add Sweetened Condensed Milk:
    • Pour in the sweetened condensed milk and stir to combine. Adjust the amount to achieve your desired level of sweetness.
    5. Prepare Iced latte:
    • Fill a serving glass with ice cubes.
    6. Add Milk:
    • Pour the milk over the ice, leaving some space at the top of the glass.
    7. Pour Taro Espresso Mixture:
    • Slowly pour the taro and espresso mixture over the milk. The taro mixture may settle at the bottom, which is normal. You can give it a gentle stir to combine.
    8. Enjoy:
    • Your Iced Taro Latte is now ready to be enjoyed.

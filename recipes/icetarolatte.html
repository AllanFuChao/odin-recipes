Ice Taro Latte

Recipe and instructions

Here's a recipe for an Iced Taro Latte, a delicious and refreshing beverage you can serve at your coffee shop:

Ingredients

    2 tablespoons of taro powder 2 shots of espresso (or 1/2 cup of strong brewed coffee) 1 cup of milk (whole milk, almond milk, or any milk of your choice) 1-2 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk (adjust to taste) 1 cup of ice cubes

Instructions:

      Brew Espresso or Coffe: If using espresso, pull 2 shots and let them cool. If using brewed coffee, make it strong and allow it to cool to room temperature.
      Mix Taro Powder: In a small bowl, mix the taro powder with a small amount of hot water to create a smooth paste. This will help the taro powder dissolve better in the drink.
